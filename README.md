# Retail Sales Analysis Dashboard

An end-to-end Excel data analysis project on a retail sales dataset,
including data cleaning, pivot tables, charts, and an interactive dashboard.

---

## Project Overview

This project analyzes retail sales data to uncover trends across product
categories, customer demographics, and time periods.

---

## Dataset

- **File:** `retail_sales_dataset.csv`
- **Fields:** Transaction ID, Date, Customer ID, Gender, Age,
  Product Category, Quantity, Price per Unit, Total Amount

---

## What Was Done

### 1. Data Cleaning
- Removed duplicates and handled missing values
- Standardized date formats
- Created Age Group buckets (Under 20, 21-30, 31-40, 41-50, 51+)

### 2. Pivot Tables & Charts
| Sheet | Analysis |
|---|---|
| Sales_by_gender | Total sales split by Female / Male |
| Sales_by_month | Monthly sales trend (2023) |
| Product Cat | Sales & quantity by product category |
| Avg_pur_by_age_Group | Average purchase value per age group |

### 3. Dashboard
A single-sheet dashboard with:
- **KPI Cards:** Total Sales ($456,000) · Total Quantity (2,514) ·
  Average Purchase ($456) · Top Category (Electronics)
- **Charts:** Monthly Sales Trend · Sales by Gender ·
  Sales by Product Category · Quantity by Category ·
  Average Purchase by Age Group

---

## Key Findings

- **Electronics** is the top-selling category ($156,905)
- **Clothing** leads in quantity sold (894 units)
- Sales peaked in **May** and dipped in **March**
- **Customers under 20** have the highest average purchase value ($551)
- **Female customers** slightly outspend male customers
